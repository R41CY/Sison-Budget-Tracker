💰 Recording Transactions

Income = Money coming INTO your business (sales, payments received)

Expense = Money going OUT of your business (supplies, rent, utilities)

Example Income: "Sold 20 pieces of Leche Flan" - ₱1,000
Example Expense: "Bought eggs at SM Hypermarket" - ₱450
💡 Tip: Record transactions daily so you don't forget. Use clear descriptions!

🧾 Recipe Costing - Real Example

Here's how to calculate if your Leche Flan business is profitable:

🍮 Leche Flan (10 cups) Selling Price: ₱85 per cup
Ingredients (based on Philippine prices):
Egg yolks12 pcs₱84.00
Condensed milk1 can (300ml)₱65.00
Evaporated milk1 can (370ml)₱45.00
White sugar (caramel)200g₱20.00
Vanilla extract1 tsp₱15.00
Llanera cups10 pcs₱50.00
LPG gas usageestimate₱25.00
TOTAL COST₱304.00
Cost per cup: ₱304 ÷ 10 = ₱30.40
Selling price: ₱85.00
Profit per cup: ₱85 - ₱30.40 = ₱54.60
Profit margin: 64.2% ✅ Excellent!
If you sell all 10: 10 × ₱54.60 = ₱546.00 profit
💡 Target: Aim for 40-60% profit margin. Above 60% is excellent! Below 30% means you need to adjust prices or costs.

🥟 Another Example: Siopao Asado

Siopao Asado (15 pieces) Selling: ₱25 each

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data saved?

All data is stored on your device's browser. It's private and secure. No internet needed after first load.

How do I backup my data?

Click "Export Backup" in the sidebar. Do this weekly to avoid losing data.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes! The app works on any device - phone, tablet, or computer.

How do I know if I'm profitable?

Check the Dashboard. If "Net Profit" is positive (green), you're making money!

✅ Best Practices for Sison Business

  1. Record transactions DAILY - Don't wait until week end
  2. Use clear descriptions - "Sold 5 Leche Flan to Ate Mila" is better than "Sales"
  3. Check Dashboard WEEKLY - Every Sunday, review your week
  4. Update recipe costs - When ingredient prices change
  5. Export backup MONTHLY - Save your data safely
  6. Track ALL expenses - Including small ones like jeepney fare for deliveries
